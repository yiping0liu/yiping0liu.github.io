Short Biography

Dr. Yiping Liu is currently a research assistant professor at Osaka Prefecture University, Japan. He received his Dr. Eng. and B. Eng. degrees from China University of Mining and Technology in 2017 and 2012, respectively. He was a visiting scholar at Oklahoma State University, USA during 2016-2017.

Evolutionary Computation, Machine Learning, Optimization
